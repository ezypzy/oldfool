About Boxfool

 

Remember that amazing feeling of anticipation when you unbox a present? It's what Boxfool tries to re-create with a new subscription service that sends you a box of surprise every 3 months.

You have no idea what you are getting - except for its theme. The items are curated by a Boxstar - specially invited individuals whom we trust will be able to surprise you!

Our first Boxfool of Eco is now available, curated by celebrity yoga instructor Ninie Ahmad.

Subscription costs RM60 / quarter, delivered right to your doorstep.
Boxfool is currently available to subscribers within the Klang Valley only.

In a Nutshell


Boxfool is a subscription service that sends you a pack of awesome goodies every 3 months.

Each Boxfool costs RM60 / quarter.

The contents of the Box remain a secret - until it arrives at your doorstep!

Boxfools are released according to themes - each is curated by a Boxstar - our panel of influential individuals who are interesting, cultured and have good taste.

Our first theme: Boxfool of Eco, curated by Ninie Ahmad. Check it out!
